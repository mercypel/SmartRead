## Features

### 📚 Smart Library
Upload and manage reading materials in `.txt`, `.docx`, and `.pdf` formats. All processing happens in the browser for privacy-focused usage.

### 📖 Multimodal Reading
Four flexible study modes:

- **Text Mode** – distraction-free reading with notes and highlights  
- **Audio Mode** – text-to-speech narration with playback controls  
- **Visual Mode** – AI-assisted slide presentation with spoken explanations
- **Game Mode** - Flashcards, Fill-in-the-gap exercises and Concept matching games  

### 📘 Dictionary & Learning Support

- instant word definitions  
- pronunciation support  
- synonyms and examples  
- contextual learning assistance  

### 🤖 Intelligent Assistant

Students can ask questions about uploaded reading materials, request summaries, explanations, and revision support.

### ✅ Comprehension Quiz

Auto-generated quizzes with instant feedback, scores, and achievement rewards.

### 📊 Progress Tracking

XP points, reading streaks, achievements, and leaderboard features encourage consistency.

### 🎯 Focus Mode

A distraction-reduced full-screen study environment.

### ✏️ Notes & Highlights

Persistent note-taking and text highlighting during reading sessions.
